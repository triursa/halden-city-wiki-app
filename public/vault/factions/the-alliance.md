## Founding

In 2015, Ethan Roberts (Paragon) brokered a coalition between himself, Recluse, Riftfire, Quantum, Starfall, and Natalia Orlov (The Vanguard — freshly defected from A.E.G.I.S.'s covert program). A tenuous knowledge-sharing framework was established with A.E.G.I.S. Regional Command: cooperation without subordination.

The same year, Joseph Persico — the Ironworks' most powerful crime boss — was convicted and imprisoned. The Alliance's founding year was also a significant street-level win.

Calypso attended the Alliance's first public address uninvited. She watched from the harbor and left without speaking to anyone.

## Culture & Feel

The Alliance is relational where A.E.G.I.S. is institutional. It is held together by shared values, history, and trust rather than org charts and oversight. Members tend to be more independent, more self-directed, and more comfortable operating without a net.

It operates as a chosen family — but chosen family means real weight when someone leaves or changes. Riley's transition to A.E.G.I.S. sponsorship changed something. He still uses the word "family." It's a marker of what that time meant and still means to him.

---

## Relationship with A.E.G.I.S.

Cooperative without being subordinate. The knowledge-sharing framework is real but limited. A.E.G.I.S. watches the Alliance closely and is not fully comfortable with its independence. Aegis Prime's presence as an ideological A.E.G.I.S. proxy within the team is a source of ongoing friction.
