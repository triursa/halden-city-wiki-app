## What Vane Capital Is

A private infrastructure firm with a near-monopoly on construction contracts in the Crownpoint District. Publicly, it is a success story: a self-made billionaire from the Ironworks who built something that transformed the city's skyline.

The growth is real. The methods behind it are not entirely legal. Vane is a Kingpin-class operator — the philanthropic persona is genuine, and so is the criminal infrastructure underneath it. He is not a front for something else. He built this himself.

He is also an Eclipse customer and ideological partner. Not a lieutenant. An early adopter who saw what PROMETHEUS was building and decided it aligned with his vision for the city. His investment in Eclipse is knowing and deliberate. His civic sincerity is real. So is his complicity.

## How the Growth Happened

Vane Capital's rise isn't explained by Eclipse money alone — it predates the PROMETHEUS relationship. The foundation is a self-built criminal network in the Ironworks that created leverage, cash flow, and silence where it was needed. The Eclipse partnership accelerated the legitimate side and gave him access to resources that made certain problems permanently soluble.

He built something that lasts. He's right about that. He's wrong about what it costs.
