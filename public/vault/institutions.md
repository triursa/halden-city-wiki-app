# 🏛️ Institutions — Halden City

Key organizations shaping the city's power structure.

---

## A.E.G.I.S.
*Anomalous Entity & Gifted Individual Surveillance*

Founded **1925** by executive order, buried in the War Department. Originally tasked with cataloguing, containing, and neutralizing anomalous human phenomena. The Crale family (old money, East Coast) was present at founding.

**Current structure (post-2010 restructuring):**
- Regional Director: **Evelyn Shaw**
- Divisions: MTR, ACAD, SMAB, MANIFEST
- Classification arm: **SEIB** (enhanced individual taxonomy — currently on Version 8, adopted 2018)

**Key tensions:**
- Maintains cooperation-without-subordination framework with the Alliance
- Runs a parallel private asset program (Operation MANIFEST / Scythe) as a god-killer contingency
- Is aware of Riley Thomas's physical decline through independent channels — he thinks he's hiding it
- Has not shared its Glass District statistical clustering analysis with the Alliance

**The Crale Connection:** Edmund Crale (current Eclipse CEO) carries institutional knowledge his family accumulated across a century of A.E.G.I.S. involvement. This is how Eclipse/PROMETHEUS has remained invisible.

---

## The Alliance
Formally established **2015**. Brokered by Paragon. Members: Paragon, Recluse, Riftfire, Quantum, Starfall, The Vanguard.

Operates on a tenuous knowledge-sharing framework with A.E.G.I.S. — cooperation without subordination. The Alliance represents the moral counterweight to A.E.G.I.S.'s institutional authority.

---

## Eclipse / PROMETHEUS
Eclipse operates publicly as a **legitimate biotech company**. Underneath: PROMETHEUS — a black program developing:
- Enhanced individual deterrence
- Controlled synthetic human assets
- Manufactured enhanced powers (reverse-engineered from existing individuals)

CEO: **Edmund Crale** (Crale family, A.E.G.I.S. founding lineage).
Early investing partner: **Casimir Vane**.
Unclosed asset: **Rhys/Noc**.

Has never appeared in any public filing or leaked document. The Virek Papers don't touch it.

---

## Vane Capital Group
Founded **2008** by Casimir Vane (Ironworks origin). Near-monopoly on Crownpoint construction contracts — growth backed by Eclipse, though not publicly known. Vane is a knowing ideological participant in PROMETHEUS, not a passive investor.

Currently: Vane is out on bail following the warehouse incident, throwing fundraiser galas for the DA. Brazen open operation while under indictment.

---

## The Persico Family
Organized crime. Filled the Ironworks vacuum after **1987** deindustrialization. Joseph Persico convicted and imprisoned **2015** — released recently and re-establishing his Dockside network.
