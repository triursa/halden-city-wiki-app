**Power origin:** Category IIIa — Glass District lab explosion (shared with Jason)

**Key thread:** Her telepathy cracked the SHADE constructs at the press conference — she confirmed they're built from human bodies, commissioned by Casimir Vane, controlled by Signal. The team has this intel. It is not yet public.
**Personal seed:** Aubrey texted her — "I saw Carter last week." Aubrey is an unwitting Signal-adjacent witness.

## Jason / Apollo
**Power origin:** Category IIIa — Glass District electromagnetic incident (2022), shared with Mary Matthews
**Original ability:** Photokinesis (light manipulation)
**Compound:** After Mary/Artemis died, Jason absorbed her shadow manipulation. Two power sets, one host — unstable. FLAG: COMPOUND + DETERIORATING.
**Wavelength:** Jason is bonded to High Spectrum; Mary was bonded to Low Spectrum. They were drawn together by the Wavelengths' ecology — neither understood why.
**Personal thread:** Lily Matthews (Mary's 15-year-old sister) texted him. She is not a mission. She is a person.

## Sterling Slate / Cosmic Knight
**Origin:** Half-Umbraeon, engineered by Darkstar. Category V (hybrid).
**Abilities:** Dark matter manipulation
**Current pressure:** Raze (his half-sibling, also Darkstar's engineered child) is hunting him — originally to retrieve him, now for revenge after Darkstar's ship was destroyed. Sterling and Raze are mirror figures: both lost Darkstar, moving in opposite directions.
**Kiara:** His Personal Idol Respect slot. She left Earth after Signal's broadcast blew her cover. She left Sterling a message. Asynchronous contact ruling applies.
