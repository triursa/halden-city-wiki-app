## Powers

**Entangled with the underlying structure of reality.** Since the 2006 accident, Javier Mendez exists in a state that requires a specially engineered containment suit to hold him in singular form outside controlled environments.

- Teleportation
- Matter phasing (between states of density)
- Accelerated cognition
- Limited duplication — extended use causes local reality instability; the longer he maintains it, the worse the environment around him becomes

---

## Background

Glass District physicist. Spent years obsessively working to prove reality could be harnessed through quantum manipulation. In 2006, the experiment succeeded catastrophically. His particles scattered across multiple quantum states simultaneously. A.E.G.I.S. spent months constructing the containment suit that allows him to exist in singular form.

He serves the Alliance as both field asset and scientific resource. He treats almost everything — conflict, morality, people — as engineering problems waiting for solutions. This is not coldness. It is how he has learned to function since 2006.

---

## Character Profile

Brilliant and contained. Rarely engages in conversations beyond the task at hand. The suit marks him as different in every room he enters, which means he has never had the option of being ordinary in public since the accident. Whether that has made him more or less present to the people around him is unclear.

**The Avi thread:** Javier Mendez is Avi Park's biological father. He departed before Avi was born and does not know Avi exists. They are now on the same team. He has not made this connection. The slow build of this relationship — before and after any revelation — belongs to the players. Do not accelerate it.
