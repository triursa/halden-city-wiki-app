## Background

Declan Harrow operates in the parts of Halden City the enhanced individual framework doesn't have language for. Not aliens. Not lab accidents. Not cosmic forces. The older things: living in the forgotten infrastructure, the deep tunnels, the buildings that change hands too often.

He has been doing this for years, alone. No sponsorship. No announcement. No press profile, no confirmed sightings, no public file.

What the dockworkers at Blackwater Harbor know: something watches the waterfront at night. Whatever it is, it's on their side. Augie Ferreira leaves a light on.

He has never claimed to be a hero. He is a hunter. The distinction matters to him.

**The A.E.G.I.S. file situation:** The redaction and misfiling of his file is deliberate. Whether this reflects institutional politics, a classified arrangement, or something A.E.G.I.S. decided was better not to acknowledge is not established. But it is not bureaucratic sloppiness.
