## Powers

**Kinetic and potential energy manipulation.** Manifested at 15.

- Short-range teleportation (redirected potential energy — moves herself out of harm's path by converting potential into kinetic displacement)
- Force redirection — can take the potential energy of any object and redirect it into kinetic output, including other people
- High-speed locomotion
- Self-limiting by choice; her true ceiling has never been formally established. She has never pushed it.

The manifestation story: she "borrowed" a friend's parents' car at 15, lost control, and was suddenly standing on the shoulder of the road unharmed. She spent years quietly training in private. She told no one.

---

## Character Profile

She came up in Riverside Ward. She knows the neighborhood the way you know a place that formed you — its rhythms, its residents, its history. She teaches physics here by day. This is not a cover; it is a deliberate choice about where she puts her daylight hours.

She brought Jason into the new team. She knows him as a student first. The shift to mentor is a dynamic in progress.

She has never established what her power ceiling actually is. Whether that is wisdom or avoidance is an open question.
