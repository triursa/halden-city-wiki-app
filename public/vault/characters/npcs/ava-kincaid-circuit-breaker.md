## Powers

- **Electrokinesis** — generates, absorbs, and directs electrical current
- **Technology manipulation** — interfaces with and overrides electronic systems
- **Power line travel** — can move through electrical infrastructure

She reappeared two days after the incident inside a downtown server room with no memory of the interval. What she was doing — or becoming — in that gap is unknown.

---

## Background

Working two jobs and drowning in debt, Ava was skimming small amounts from unsecured ATMs using stolen firmware — nothing flashy, just enough to survive. She hit the wrong machine in Riverside Ward. The ATM had been retrofitted by a Glass District contractor testing next-generation biometric encryption and adaptive voltage security.

The surge crossed three city blocks. Traffic lights died. Phones bricked. Power lines arced blue. Ava was gone.

She started as a low-level opportunist. Her ambitions have grown since. Whether that trajectory ends somewhere interesting or somewhere destructive is still being decided.
