## Who He Is

Owen Watson is not stupid. He found a framework that explained his pain and stopped looking. His reactive transformation — triggered by romantic rejection, public humiliation, or ideological confrontation — produces a massive, hyper-muscular form with extreme strength, rapid regeneration, and durability that scales with his anger.

More dangerous than his personal power: in transformed state, he emits an emotional amplification field that intensifies nearby insecurity and resentment, making crowds more volatile and making the team's own dynamics visible under pressure.

When the fight ends and the form drops, he's just Owen again. That part is worth playing.

---

## Tactical Notes

- Most dangerous in public spaces with audiences
- Amplification field is a genuine crowd-scene complication
- Two conditions means he resolves fast — use him as a complication, not an anchor
- Triggered by disrespect or rejection from PCs — target the one who doesn't take him seriously
