## Alliance Members

### Riley Thomas / Recluse
**Age:** 35 · **AEGIS File:** AEGIS-RCL-002
Spider-derived genetics. Powers: wall adhesion, enhanced agility, enhanced strength, **one-second precognitive flash**. Web via mechanical cartridges.

Trained as an unsanctioned vigilante for ~15 years before formal recognition in 2015. Founded on guilt: the Vega Incident (2006) — a fire alarm Riley pulled during a robbery caused Sandra Vega's death. Her 2-year-old son Adrian survived. Riley has visited her grave annually ever since. That same Adrian Vega is now his mentee and the most tactically precise partnership in the Alliance network.

**The decline:** Riley is physically deteriorating — slower healing, stiff joints. Hiding it from A.E.G.I.S. medical staff. He believes it's a secret. It is not — Evelyn Shaw has clinical intelligence on it, sourced via Casey Holt/Scythe through Adrian.

**The blind spot:** His precognitive flash has a documented gap around Casey Holt/Scythe. He doesn't know it. A.E.G.I.S. does. Casey is Adrian's girlfriend.

**The recording:** Signal recorded Riley threatening Carter Williams at the hospital — off-books, alone. Signal is holding it as his endgame weapon against Recluse's moral authority.

### Ethan Roberts / Paragon
**Age:** ~45 · Current host of the Paragon Force.
Air Force pilot who bonded to the Force mid-air during a residual energy crisis in 2005. Established cooperation-without-subordination with A.E.G.I.S. Brokered the Alliance in 2015.

Signal's endgame: corner him into voluntarily releasing the Paragon Force at Monument Circle by broadcasting that he *can* release it and chose not to. Paragon's arc ends in self-sacrifice, not defeat.

### Natalia Orlov / The Vanguard
First-generation MANIFEST product (Operation MANIFEST, 1953). Neural implants, gene-locked muscle density, bioelectric integration, pain suppression, uploaded combat training. Built as a god-killer contingency — the state asset designed to neutralize a rogue Paragon.

Defected from A.E.G.I.S. She did not know they ran MANIFEST again (Casey Holt) without her. The parallel is load-bearing — she left because she saw the institution clearly; Casey stayed for the same reason Natalia left.

Arc 1 end: A.E.G.I.S. detains Natalia under crisis authority. Seeds Arc 2's institutional overreach.

---

### Kiara Je-rach / Starfall
Engineered cosmic envoy of the Auditors. Original mission: assess Earth for removal from the cosmic record. Defected after Paragon asked her what she believed. Now marked "defective" by her creators.

**Status: Off-Earth.** Signal's broadcast blew her cover in Session 1. She departed on her own terms — left Sterling a message expressing belief in him. Her arc is load-bearing for Arc 3: if the Auditors return, the conviction she formed has to hold without Paragon to back it.

*Sterling's Personal Idol Respect slot. Asynchronous contact ruling active.*

### Sloane Callahan / Arachne
**Age:** 21 · Intel/surveillance operator.
Not enhanced in the traditional sense — her capabilities are technical and cognitive. Runs Weaver drone surveillance covering half of Riverside Ward and most of Dockside. Perfect memory (unexamined — the story may eventually disagree with her assumption that it's just how her brain works).

Found A.E.G.I.S. research servers embarrassingly easy to hack at 19. Riley showed up in her doorway and offered her a role instead of an arrest. She still isn't sure why she said yes.

**Active thread:** Her drones are flagging a recurring EM interference pattern in Riverside Ward/Ironworks correlating with Petra Mace/Sable. She hasn't told Riley yet.

## A.E.G.I.S. Personnel

### Evelyn Shaw — Regional Director
Post-2010 restructuring. Expanded surveillance and detention authority. Authorized the second-generation MANIFEST program (Casey Holt) without Natalia's knowledge or Lila Ortega's knowledge. Running Casey as insurance against Riley while maintaining a cooperative framework with him. The silence is institutional self-protection dressed as operational necessity.

### Lila Ortega — MANIFEST Director
**Age:** 39 · Reports to Shaw.
Warm, relationship-based approach to new manifestations — a contact, not a handler. Saved by the team in Session 1. **Team now has influence over her.**

What she can provide: MANIFEST files, internal A.E.G.I.S. access, Shaw's ear, buffer against ETR escalation.
What she doesn't know: Casey Holt/Scythe exists. A.E.G.I.S. ran a second-gen MANIFEST asset through Shaw, bypassing Lila entirely.

## Antagonists

### Signal
**Type:** Ordinary human. Former Renegade Force host (voluntarily relinquished — only confirmed case where a host can end the bond unilaterally and survive).

Operates entirely on preparation, patience, and the knowledge that the team will make mistakes he can film. Never sounds angry. Places a question and lets it grow. His primary weapon: the hospital recording of Riley threatening Carter Williams.

**Drive:** Prove that heroism is a power structure like any other, and watch it collapse.
**Arc 1 end state:** Escapes. Real name and Renegade history remain unrevealed. Not resolved in this arc.

→ Full files: `halden-city-wiki/characters/npcs/anonymous-signal.md` · `halden-city-wiki/plot/villain-sheets/signal.md`

### Carter Williams / Renegade
Current host of the Renegade Force. Has a **Guilty condition** — the thread that makes him reachable at Monument Circle. Avi's personal thread. Signal recorded Riley threatening him at the hospital.

### Casimir Vane
Ironworks-origin entrepreneur. Knowing Eclipse ideological participant — not a passive investor. Commissioned the SHADE construct bodies. Currently out on bail, throwing a DA fundraiser gala. The team has Avi's intel connecting him to the constructs. He doesn't know what they know.

### Darkstar
Umbraeon explorer. Near-immortal. Has been stress-testing Halden City since 2012 — empowering unstable individuals, introducing experimental bioagents, engineering scenarios. Believes suffering accelerates evolution. His ship was destroyed; survival unconfirmed (working assumption: wounded but alive, building a deterrent weapon to permanently end the Paragon/Renegade Force cycle).

Engineered both Sterling (half-Umbraeon, defected) and Raze (Vorrkai hybrid, active revenge plot). The Sterling situation complicates his methodology in ways not yet known.

**The parasite:** Introduced into Riley Thomas ~10 years ago — slow-acting, targets the spider gene, designed to strip moral restraint. Riley's physical decline is a side effect. Darkstar is monitoring passively.

### Raze
Darkstar's engineered child. Vorrkai/Umbraeon hybrid. Was off Darkstar's ship when it was destroyed. Assumes Darkstar is dead. Original mission (retrieve Sterling) is now a revenge plot. Has license to kill. Sterling and Raze are mirror figures — both lost Darkstar, moving in opposite directions.

## Casey Holt / Scythe
**⚠️ GM-ONLY information — do not surface to players**

Second-generation MANIFEST asset. Built as A.E.G.I.S.'s contingency specifically for the day Recluse becomes a liability. Conditioned from childhood to produce no threat-state signals — Riley's precognitive flash returns nothing around her. The blind spot is not a power; it is architecture.

Cover: nursing student at Halden University, part-time EMT. Currently dating **Adrian Vega** (~2 months at campaign start). Adrian told her Riley is Recluse. She reported it. Adrian doesn't know. The breach of trust is complete before the campaign begins.

She believes in the mission. Her certainty has never been tested by actual deployment. Whether conviction and action are the same for her is the unresolved question.

Neither Lila Ortega nor Natalia Orlov know she exists.
