# 🌍 Lore — Halden City

World history and metahuman taxonomy summaries.

## Timeline Highlights

### Era I: The Secret Age (1925–1959)
- **1925** — A.E.G.I.S. founded in secrecy. Crale family present at founding.
- **1940** — The Paragon Force first documented ("Asset CROWN" — fights with Allied forces).
- **1947** — Renegade Force first recorded: Soviet-adjacent operative mirroring and countering Asset CROWN.
- **1953** — Operation MANIFEST authorized. Goal: produce a state asset capable of neutralizing a rogue Paragon. (Natalia Orlov is the eventual product.)
- **1958** — Halden City's first public superhuman incident. A.E.G.I.S. clears it in four hours.

### Era II: The Public Age (1960–1984)
- **1963** — A.E.G.I.S. authorizes Paragon to operate semi-publicly.
- **1968** — Glass District established. Stated: medical/materials research. Actual: enhanced individual study, power exploitation. Ethical breaches begin almost immediately.
- **1979** — Calypso makes contact with the previous Paragon during a North Atlantic crisis. Uneasy accord. A.E.G.I.S. creates a new file classification for an immortal mythological entity.
- **1982** — Dr. Gabriel Virek begins assembling the Virek Papers: classified evidence of Paragon's extralegal interventions.

### Era III: Crisis & Consequence (1985–1999)
- **1987** — Ironworks collapse. Persico family fills the vacuum. Street-level enhanced activity rises.
- **1999** — *The West Coast Broadcast.* Nemesis (Virek/Renegade Force) engineers a televised confrontation with Paragon. Both die. Paragon Force goes dormant. Renegade Force dissipates. Riley Thomas (age 9) watches from Riverside Ward. Ethan Roberts (18) enlists in the Air Force the following year.

### Era IV: The New Generation (2000–2015)
- **2005** — Ethan Roberts bonds to the Paragon Force mid-air. Survives a crash that should have killed him.
- **2006** — Riley Thomas (16) manifests. The Vega Incident: Sandra Vega is killed, Adrian found unharmed. Riley goes underground.
- **2006** — Javier Mendez/Quantum experiment collapses catastrophically.
- **2008** — Casimir Vane founds Vane Capital Group. Eclipse backing undisclosed.
- **2009** — Kiara Je-rach arrives. Defects after Paragon asks her what she believes.
- **2010** — A.E.G.I.S. restructured under Evelyn Shaw. Second-generation MANIFEST authorized.
- **2012** — Darkstar makes first documented Halden City contact.
- **2015** — The Alliance formally established. Joseph Persico convicted.
- **2022** — Glass District electromagnetic incident: Jason and Mary Matthews manifest. High Spectrum and Low Spectrum bond simultaneously — the Wavelengths.
- **2022** — Wave of manifestations: Nightfall, Red Pill, Wraith, Carrion.
- **2023** — Breakpoint (Adrian Vega) trained and active. Requiem (Marcus Webb) killed and resurrected via off-books procedure. Shroud (Declan Harrow) established presence at Blackwater Harbor.

### Story Present (2025–2026)
- Persico re-establishing Dockside network after release
- Three Glass District researchers missing in 8 months
- Signal's campaign active
- Riley's deterioration accelerating
- Eclipse/PROMETHEUS operational and invisible

## Metahuman Taxonomy (A.E.G.I.S. SEIB Version 8, 2018)

| Category | Definition | Examples |
|----------|-----------|---------|
| **I — Cosmic Force Bonding** | Host bonded to a non-terrestrial principle. Power passes *through* them, not *from* them. Force is the continuous entity. | Paragon (Ethan), Renegade (Carter), former: Nemesis/Virek, Signal |
| **II — Genetic Manifestation** | Power native to the host's biology. Develops rather than activates. Can be inherited. | Recluse (Riley), Requiem (Marcus), Filament (Zara) |
| **III — Experimental Exposure** | Ability from contact with anomalous materials/energies. Most can name a specific incident. | Quantum, Nova, Apollo, Aegis Prime, Circuit Breaker, Nightfall |
| **IV — Program Enhancement** | Deliberately engineered by institutional actors. | Vanguard (Natalia) — IVa; Scythe (Casey) — IVa; PROMETHEUS subjects — IVb |
| **V — Cosmic/Alien Origin** | Non-terrestrial biology or inheritance. | Starfall (Kiara) — Va; Cosmic Knight (Sterling) — Vb; Darkstar — Va |
| **VI — Mythological Channeling** | Power from ongoing bond with a distinct mythological entity. | Carrion (Allison Bryne) — champion of the Morrigan |

**Active modifier flags:**
- **DETERIORATING:** Riley/Recluse (hidden decline), Jason/Apollo (compound power instability)
- **COMPOUND:** Jason/Apollo (photokinesis + absorbed shadow manipulation — two Category III sets, unstable)
- **ENTANGLED:** Filament/Zara (spider-gene entanglement with Riley — receives his physical distress passively)

**Taxonomy gaps SEIB hasn't addressed:**
- Darkstar's experimental subjects (filed provisional Category III for 13 years)
- Eclipse/PROMETHEUS synthetic manufacture — Category IV with no oversight framework
- Shroud's phenomena — filed as Category II, almost certainly incomplete
- The Glass District clustering: statistically anomalous, not formally documented, not shared with Alliance

## The Cosmic Forces

Two paired principles, running through human history:

- **The Paragon Force** — transfers only on host death. Cannot be voluntarily relinquished.
- **The Renegade Force** — *can* be voluntarily relinquished. Only confirmed Category I case where the host survives ending the bond. Signal relinquished it; no detectable residual abilities.

Quantum's theory: the Forces are embedded cosmic principles, not independent intelligences. SEIB has neither adopted nor rejected this. If correct, it would reclassify Category I entirely.

The two Forces appear entangled as paired principles — strengthening of one historically corresponds with strengthening of the other.

---

## The Wavelengths

Ancient interdimensional beings existing as pure EM energy at the extremes of the light spectrum — High Spectrum and Low Spectrum. Bonded simultaneously to Jason and Mary Matthews during the 2022 Glass District electromagnetic incident. Their ecology draws their hosts together. Neither Jason nor any human institution recognized the significance. Mary/Artemis died; Jason absorbed her power (now unstable compound). The Wavelength connection is not documented by A.E.G.I.S.
