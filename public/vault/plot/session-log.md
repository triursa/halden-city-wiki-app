## Pre-Session — Origin Event (Weeks Before Session 1)

**What happened:**
The team acted independently before the Alliance formally introduced them to the city. They targeted Casimir Vane at a warehouse. Burnout (Daniel Rojas) was also present — his own Vane vendetta, no coordination. The scuffle caused a large explosion. The warehouse event drew significant public attention.

**Consequence:** Casimir was arrested. The Alliance was forced to reveal the player team earlier than anticipated — they had a plan for a formal handoff; the players went out on their own first.

**Who witnessed:** Raze (Sterling's half-sibling, Darkstar's engineered child) observed the event from outside.

**Current status:** Casimir is out on bail. He is throwing a fundraiser gala for the city's DA — a brazen public move while under indictment.

## Session 1

**Date played:** March 2026
**Arc beats covered:** Beat 2 (Press Conference), Beat 3 partial (Signal's first broadcast delivered mid-Beat 2)

---

### What Happened

**The press conference:**
New team formally introduced to the city. Senator Okafor present. Signal hacked all A.E.G.I.S. comms simultaneously — full blackout. SHADE constructs (cyborg cadavers in A.E.G.I.S. uniforms) attacked mid-conference and moved to extract Senator Okafor. Riley pursued and protected the Senator. Players handled the constructs.

**Avi's telepathy:**
Avi cracked the SHADE constructs' minds during the fight. Intel extracted:
- The constructs are built from human bodies
- They were commissioned by Casimir Vane
- They were being controlled by Signal at the time of the attack

**Signal's broadcast:**
Signal used the A.E.G.I.S. blackout window to broadcast Kiara Je-rach's full origin publicly:
- Her engineered nature and original mission (assess Earth for removal from cosmic record)
- Her Auditor "defective" designation
- Her barista civilian cover identity
- The internal A.E.G.I.S. transcript of their argument with Paragon over whether to trust her — in which Paragon staked his public reputation on her

**A.E.G.I.S. response:** Full lockdown. Cannot confirm scope of what was accessed.

**Lila Ortega:**
MANIFEST director was present at the conference and attacked by SHADE constructs. Players saved her. **Team now has influence over Lila Ortega.**

**End-of-session consequence:**
Kiara left Earth following the broadcast. She left Sterling a message: she explained her decision, expressed belief in him, said she wants to find her place in the universe. Her departure was a deliberate choice, not a flight.
