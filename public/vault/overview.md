# 🏙️ Halden City — World Overview

> *"The Crown of the Coast."*
> A superhero setting between New York and D.C. Used for fiction writing, TTRPG (Masks: A New Generation), and collaborative storytelling.

---

## The Setting

Halden City sits on the northeastern seaboard between New York and D.C. It is a city of layered power — old money, federal institutions, organized crime, and an increasingly visible enhanced individual community. It has been the site of superhuman activity since 1925, most of it hidden from public record until the 1960s.

### Districts at a Glance
| District | Character |
|---------|---------|
| **Crownpoint** | Financial core, federal presence, A.E.G.I.S. command tower, old money |
| **The Glass District** | Defense contractors, academic research, experimental facilities — source of many manifestations |
| **Riverside Ward** | Working class, historically underserved, Recluse's home neighborhood |
| **The Ironworks** | Deindustrialized, organized crime vacuum, street-level enhanced activity |
| **Blackwater Harbor** | Dockworker history, Persico family, Shroud's territory |
| **Monument Circle** | Civic center, public demonstrations, political flashpoint |

---

## The Tone

Inspired by *Masks: A New Generation*. The setting treats superhero institutions — A.E.G.I.S., the Alliance, the Paragon legacy — as genuine but flawed. The dramatic question across arcs is not "can we win" but "what are we becoming and who decides."
